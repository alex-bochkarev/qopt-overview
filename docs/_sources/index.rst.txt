QC for discrete optimization: code documentation.
=================================================

These supplementary materials document the numerical experiments for the
research project:

-----

.. rst-class:: center

**"Quantum computing for discrete optimization:
a highlight of three technologies"**

by A. Bochkarev, R. Heese, S. Jäger, P. Schiewe, and A. Schöbel.

-----

Here we present our computational setup, source code, and the data with format
descriptions. Please do not hesitate to `reach out
<https://www.bochkarev.io/contact>`_ if you have any further questions.

The preprint is available as `arXiv:2409.01373 <https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.01373>`_
